About me

Originally a foreign languages teacher, I embarked on my designing and coding journey because of the great frustration I felt whilst browsing websites, and realised I wanted to decode what's behind the surface and understand how this crazy thing works, and how to make it better. My curiosity has fostered a real passion for UX - things can be so much more straightforward! I believe that simplicity is the key. We are often overwhelmed by a cascade of information arriving from all angles and simply do not have time to chase buttons and links. My biggest mission is to build sites that are user friendly and search engine optimised.
I am a perfectionist and whilst it's very hard to attain such levels, I certainly strive to. Websites should look good and be functional at the same time. Why would anyone accept less?
